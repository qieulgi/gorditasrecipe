Delicious Carne Asada Gorditas

Ingredients for the Gordita

  • Maseca (corn flour)
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Warm water
  • Skirt steak
  • Garlic/Salt paste
  • Orange and Lime juice
  • Neutral olive oil (not extra virgin)
  • White vinegar
  • Spices: pepper, cumin, chili powder, Mexican oregano, onion powder
  • Seasonings: onion, cilantro, and jalapeño

Cooking Steps

When it comes to making carne asada gorditas, the process is quite simple!

  • Roast garlic cloves until soft, then grind them into a paste.
  • Add cilantro, lime juice, orange juice, vinegar, olive oil, and spices. Mix well.
  • Marinate the skirt steak in the mixture for 2-4 hours in the fridge.
  • Prepare guacamole and set aside, sealing with plastic wrap to prevent oxidation.
  • Mix maseca with warm water until dough is soft.
  • Divide dough into 8 pieces, stuff with cheese, form balls, then flatten.
  • Grill the carne asada or cook it in a cast iron skillet with onions and jalapeños.
  • Chop carne asada into small chunks and add lime juice.
  • Fry the gorditas at 325°F until golden brown.
  • Cut the gorditas halfway open and stuff them with guacamole, queso fresco, lettuce, carne asada, crema, and salsa.
  • Enjoy!
